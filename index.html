Quit Smoking Information

Benefits of Quitting Smoking

  • Improve Cardiopulmonary Function

    Quitting smoking reduces the burden on your heart and blood vessels, helps improve blood circulation, lower blood pressure, and reduces the risk of heart disease and stroke.

  • Reduce Cancer Risk

    Smoking is a major cause of many cancers. After quitting, you can lower the risk of lung cancer, oral cancer, throat cancer, and other cancers related to smoking.

  • Improve Skin Condition

    Smoking accelerates skin aging, causing wrinkles and dull complexion. After quitting smoking, your skin will have the opportunity to recover, improving texture and color.

  • Save Money

    Smoking is an expensive habit. After quitting, you can save a lot of money each year, which can be used for other health-related activities or invested in your favorite hobbies.

Methods of Quitting Smoking

There are many methods for quitting smoking. Depending on the individual's situation, you can choose the most suitable plan. Below are some common methods:

  • Cold Turkey Method: Quit smoking all at once and stop immediately.
  • Gradual Reduction Method: Gradually reduce the amount of smoking each day until you quit.
  • Medication Assistance Method: Use quit-smoking medications to help reduce withdrawal symptoms.
